From the Principal’s Desk

Shri Krishnaswamy College For Women is the embodiment of the vision of our Chairman who has assiduously fostered the growth of educational institutions to serve society’s needs.

The College has grown steadily in terms of student strength as well as in the range of courses of study that are offered. The concomitant expansion of the infrastructure and facilities has ensured that students can pursue their tertiary education in a happy and healthy environment.

The introduction of Shift - II in 2018, has been a milestone in the annals of SKCW. This initiative has been taken to provide opportunities to larger member of students. The various achievements by both the staff and students have taken the College on a path of intellectual ascent. The College is also putting in all efforts to obtain NAAC Certification. SKCW is continuously engaged in several activities to enhance the academic and Co-curricular student participation.

With the continued support and encouragement of our Chairman and the Management, we are confident that SKCW will continue to grow, remain nonpareil and gain its rightful place as a centre for educational excellence.
